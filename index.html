The Woods

It looks like a peaceful field surrounded by the woods. A river flows through the center. Is it serene or haunting? Only the future knows the results.

The Beach

It looks like you could go swimming here and it would be pretty fun. The sand is soft, tickling your feet. The sun is glowing on your skin, and the the waves gently lap against your ankles.

The Rocky Beach

It looks like you could go swimming here, but less fun. The non-existent sand is soft, if you have iron feet. Shoes would be recommended here, again, unless you have iron feet.

The Rocky Shore

It looks like you could go swimming here, but only if you really know what you're doing. One strong wave and you could hit your head into the rocks. Who knows what creatures could pull you under? Best be careful.

The Waterfall

It's dangerous to go alone. If you look behind the waterfall, you might find a secret. Could this a Zelda reference? Possibly.

The Foggy View

The fog will soon cover us. We will be blinded by the damp view, never certain of what is approaching. What mysteries await? My heart rate quickens as I realize what this means. Soon, I will be the last. Could this be... the end?

The Forest Path

The forest path is inviting you in. It is clearly trying to lead you somewhere. But something's amiss... Could the forest be trying to help you? Or something else? For a split second you think you hear something, is it human? Something more sinister? Or your imagination? You continue down the path, curious about where it leads to. Your footsteps echo into nothingness. Where did you go? Who were you again? ...I can't quite recall.
